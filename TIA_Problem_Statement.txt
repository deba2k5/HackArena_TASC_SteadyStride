Touchless Invoice Agent
Take any client's timesheet, in any format, all the way to a validated invoice in their inbox, with people stepping in only by exception.
TH E  PR O B L E M TO D AY
Invoicing TASC's contract staff is slow and manual. Every 
client sends timesheets in a different way, and the 
Financial Operations team re-keys each one, builds the 
invoices, checks them by hand, and emails them out.
Any format, any channel, including handwriting.
Heavy re-keying and manual checking.
Days to turn around, with no visibility for clients.
H O W  I T W O R K S
1 Capture   Collect timesheets from email, the client portal and the timesheet app.
2 Understand   Read typed and handwritten entries, then merge into one upload-ready file.
3 Generate   The TASC Smart Bot loads the payroll system and produces the invoices.
4 Validate   Check each invoice against the client's business rules and flag exceptions.
5 Deliver   Send in each client's preferred order and track approvals on one dashboard.
